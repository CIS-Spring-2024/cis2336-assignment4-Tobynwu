Meet the Owner

Tobenna Nwufo

Welcome to The University of Houston Sugarland Cafeteria!

I'm Tobenna, the heart and soul behind this culinary haven. Inspired by my dads homemade food kitchen hoping to make my dreams of running a food establishment a reality, I founded this cafe with a simple mission: to bring joy to every plate.

At UOH Sugarland Cafeteria, we're more than just a place to dine we are a community. From Student to passing Visitor, what's guarenteed is that each dish will carry the labor of love. Join us as we create moments of warmth, laughter, and unforgettable flavors. Welcome home. Go COOGS!!
